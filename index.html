Courses             Top Colleges

  • Top engineering colleges in India in 2021 as per NIRF Ranking include 23 IITs, 31 NITs, IIIT Hyderabad, BITS Pilani, ISM Dhanbad and many other private colleges. Some other good universities and private colleges in India include Jamia Millia Islamia, Amity Noida, Jaypee Noida, Graphic Era Dehradun and Anna University.

  • These colleges have been ranked by NIRF Ranking 2021, India Today, The Week and many other ranking agencies for the engineering courses they offer, admissions to which are done mainly through entrance exams like JEE Main and GATE.

  • There are 4804 engineering colleges in India offering BTech, MTech and PhD degrees in multiple specializations like CSE, Electronics, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Biotech, etc. These include 3982 private and 822 government engineering colleges.

  • Admissions here are mainly done through JEE Main, KCET, UPSEE and other university exams for undergraduation courses BTech and BE. For postgraduate engineering courses, GATE, TANCET, Karnataka PGCET are the most popular exams.

  • The average cost of engineering education in India is INR 2,50,000 in government colleges and INR 10-15 lakh in private colleges.

  • After completing their engineering courses, students get an average salary of INR 10-18 lakh in IITs. In other non-premier engineering colleges, the average engineering salary is around INR 3-7 lakh per year.
